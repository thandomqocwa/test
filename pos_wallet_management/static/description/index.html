Odoo POS Wallet Management

Manage wallet of customers in Odoo!!

Odoo POS Wallet Management module allows the seller to create wallets for the customer in POS Session. Customers can get their wallet recharge to add wallet credits at any time and these wallet credits can be used by the customer for future purchases.

Create a wallet for customers in POS!!
Many times, customers are unable to make payments at the time of checkout due to lack of money or failed card transactions. To make customer's payment convenient and secure, Odoo POS Wallet Management Module comes in action to make the shopping experience easy at your POS store.

With the help of this module, the seller does not need to take payment as cash every time, he can use customer wallet credits as payment. This module is best for regular customers i.e Customer can recharge their wallet at any time in POS Session and use their wallet credits for future payment.

Highlighted Features of POS Multi Pricelist

Detailed Features List

Create POS wallets for customers in Odoo

  • POS user can create and recharge a wallet for a customer from the Point of Sale Session.
  • A POS user can recharge the wallet using wallet id also.

Maintain the translation history at the Odoo backend

  • The transaction history of the wallet payment will be maintained at Odoo Backend.
  • A POS user can also recharge or use the wallet in Offline mode.
  • Module works even when the POS is offline.
  • POS Session View
  • Backend View

On clicking 'Create & Recharge' button, wallet is automatically created and seller will be redirected to the recharge screen to proceed to recharge.
